# Cuno Design V2 Moodboard Translation

## Visual Thesis

Midnight strategy-lab design with deep navy surfaces, ultraviolet and cobalt glow, soft glass panels, and abstract luminous forms instead of generic business layout blocks.

1. Hero poster with strongest brand presence and one dominant visual stage
2. Service band showing the three Cuno support routes

4. Final CTA and FAQ in a sharper dark-glass treatment

## Interaction Thesis

- slow ambient glow drift in the hero background
- subtle reveal motion as sections enter view
- hover brightening on service panels and glass buttons

## Moodboard Signals Applied

- dark navy and black foundation instead of light canvas
- electric violet, cobalt blue, and neon pink as the accent system
- Space Grotesk-led typographic feel with bigger, cleaner poster headlines
- glassmorphism and glow-edge panels
- abstract orbits, halos, and chart lines instead of stock imagery
- stronger contrast and fewer generic content boxes
